How do I use Lekhak Sathi to convert handwritten text into digital form?

Navigate to the Upload section of our website then click on the 'Upload File' button and select the image of your handwritten document.
Once the image is uploaded, click on 'Convert' to start the OCR process.
After the conversion is complete, you can view the text in the output box. You will also have the option to download it as a .docx file by clicking on the 'Download' button.

What types of files can I upload for conversion?

Lekhak Sathi supports various image file formats including JPG and PNG. Make sure your file is clear and the handwriting is legible to ensure the best accuracy in conversion.

How accurate is the text conversion?

The accuracy of text conversion largely depends on the clarity and quality of the handwritten document uploaded. Our OCR technology is tailored for Nepali scripts and has been trained with diverse handwriting samples to handle variations in style. However, complex or extremely cursive handwriting might affect the accuracy. We continuously work on improving our OCR technology to handle a wider range of handwriting styles.

Can I convert documents in other languages besides Nepali?

Currently, Lekhak Sathi specializes in the Nepali language. We are planning to expand our services to include other scripts in the future. Keep an eye on our updates for more information.

What should I do if the converted text contains errors?

While we strive for high accuracy in text conversion, some errors may occur, especially with challenging handwriting styles. If you notice errors in the converted text then you can manually correct them in the output box before downloading the document.
